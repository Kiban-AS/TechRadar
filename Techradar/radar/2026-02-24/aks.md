[Azure Kubernetes Service (AKS)](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/aks/) is Microsoft Azure's managed Kubernetes offering that simplifies the deployment, management, and scaling of containerized applications. AKS handles critical tasks such as health monitoring, upgrades, and node provisioning, reducing the operational overhead of running Kubernetes in production. AKS is the primary platform for delivering Kubernetes capabilities at Kiban, with a strong focus on automation, GitOps, governance, and developer enablement. It supports multi-cluster, high-availability deployments and integrates natively with Azure services including Key Vault, Container Registry, Policy, and Active Directory. AKS is the recommended Kubernetes runtime for Azure-based platform engineering teams.
