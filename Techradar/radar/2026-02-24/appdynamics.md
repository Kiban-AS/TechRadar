[AppDynamics](https://www.appdynamics.com/) is a Cisco-owned application performance management (APM) and observability platform that provides deep code-level visibility into application performance, business transactions, and infrastructure health. AppDynamics has been used to monitor backend applications, diagnose code-level failures, monitor business transactions between end users and remote services, and create operational dashboards. While AppDynamics provides powerful APM capabilities, teams building cloud-native Kubernetes platforms should prefer newer observability solutions such as Dynatrace, Datadog, or the open-source PLG stack, which integrate more naturally with containerized workloads and are more cost-effective at scale. AppDynamics may be continued in existing projects but should not be chosen for new observability initiatives.
