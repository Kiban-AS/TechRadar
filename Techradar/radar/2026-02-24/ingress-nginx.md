[Ingress NGINX](https://kubernetes.github.io/ingress-nginx/) is the most widely used Kubernetes ingress controller, based on NGINX, that manages external HTTP and HTTPS traffic routing to services within a Kubernetes cluster. It implements the Kubernetes Ingress resource and supports advanced features including TLS termination, path-based and host-based routing, rate limiting, authentication, and custom NGINX configuration. Ingress NGINX is a mature, battle-tested ingress solution with broad community support and extensive documentation. It integrates well with cert-manager for automated TLS certificate management. Ingress NGINX is the recommended ingress controller for most Kubernetes deployments where a lightweight, well-understood HTTP reverse proxy is needed, and is particularly well suited for teams already familiar with NGINX configuration.
