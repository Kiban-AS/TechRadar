[Splunk](https://www.splunk.com/) is an enterprise-grade data platform for searching, monitoring, and analyzing machine-generated data including logs, metrics, and events from any source. Splunk provides powerful SPL (Search Processing Language) for ad-hoc log analysis, real-time alerting, dashboards, and SIEM capabilities. Splunk has been used for monitoring and analysis of infrastructure logs, creating custom alert policies and SLA-aligned dashboards. While Splunk is a powerful and mature platform, teams should evaluate whether its cost profile is justified compared to the open-source ELK stack or the PLG (Prometheus, Loki, Grafana) observability stack for their specific use case. Splunk is best suited for organizations with existing investment or strong security/compliance requirements for log management.
