[OpenTofu](https://opentofu.org/) is an open-source fork of Terraform, maintained by the Linux Foundation, created in response to HashiCorp's license change. It is fully compatible with existing Terraform configurations and providers, making it a drop-in replacement with a community-driven governance model. OpenTofu has been used to model and automate GitLab configuration and access as code, enabling consistent, controlled, and auditable infrastructure changes. Teams evaluating alternatives to commercial Terraform licensing should take a close look at OpenTofu as a viable and actively maintained replacement.
