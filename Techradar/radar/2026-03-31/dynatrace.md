[Dynatrace](https://www.dynatrace.com/) is an enterprise-grade AI-powered observability and application performance monitoring platform. It provides full-stack observability including infrastructure monitoring, application performance management (APM), digital experience monitoring, and AIOps capabilities through its Davis AI engine. Dynatrace has been evaluated as a proof-of-concept for Kubernetes cluster monitoring and application observability, with users onboarded to the platform and initial dashboards established. While Dynatrace offers powerful automated root cause analysis and deep Kubernetes integration, teams should assess whether its enterprise cost is justified compared to the open-source PLG stack (Prometheus, Loki, Grafana) for their specific observability needs.
