[Kyverno](https://kyverno.io/) is a policy engine designed for Kubernetes that allows platform teams to define, manage, and enforce policies as Kubernetes resources. Unlike OPA/Gatekeeper, Kyverno does not require a separate policy language — policies are written as Kubernetes manifests and stored in version control. Kyverno is used to maintain and govern AKS policy and access control, enforce platform guardrails, standardize security controls across namespaces and environments, and manage certification. It significantly reduces the risk of misconfiguration and improves efficiency when onboarding new teams and workloads, ensuring traceability through peer reviews and version control.
