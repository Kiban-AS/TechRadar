[Dependency-Track](https://dependencytrack.org/) is an intelligent component analysis platform developed by OWASP that continuously monitors applications and their dependencies for known vulnerabilities using Software Bill of Materials (SBOM). It ingests CycloneDX and SPDX SBOMs produced during CI/CD builds and correlates them against vulnerability databases such as NVD, OSV, and GitHub Advisories to identify and prioritize risks. Dependency-Track provides a continuous, portfolio-wide view of dependency health across all applications, enabling security teams to track and remediate vulnerabilities over time rather than just at build time. It integrates with CI/CD pipelines, Jira, and notification systems for automated alerting. Dependency-Track is recommended for organizations maturing their DevSecOps practices and needing centralized SBOM management and continuous vulnerability monitoring beyond point-in-time pipeline scans.
