[Datadog](https://www.datadoghq.com/) is a cloud-scale monitoring and analytics platform that provides full-stack observability including infrastructure monitoring, APM, log management, real user monitoring, and security monitoring in a unified SaaS platform. Datadog integrates with over 600 technologies and cloud providers, providing out-of-the-box dashboards and alerting. Datadog has been used for system performance monitoring, custom alerting, and notification delivery from infrastructure events, with Terraform integration for managing dashboards and alert configurations as code. Teams evaluating commercial observability platforms should compare Datadog with Dynatrace and the open-source PLG stack based on their feature needs, team scale, and cost constraints.
